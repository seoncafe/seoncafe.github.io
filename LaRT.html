LaRT (Lyman-alpha Radiative Tranfer)
  • LaRT is a cell-based, 3D Monte-Carlo RT code for the Lyman-alpha radiation, written in modern fortran. The code has been developed to study the Wouthuysen-Field effect. LaRT can also calculate polarization by fully taking into account the fine-structure of neutral hydrogen.
  • The result will be published soon. A keynote presented in Vietnam is available for download (click here).
  • LaRT: Monte Carlo Simulation of the WF effect (Seon & Kim, 2020, to be submitted)
