LaRT (Lyman-alpha Radiative Trasnfer)

LaRT is a cell-based, 3D Monte-Carlo RT code for the Lyɑ radiation, written in modern fortran.

The code has been developed to study Lyɑ RT and the Wouthuysen-Field effect. LaRT can also calculate polarization by fully taking into account the fine-structure of neutral hydrogen.

A keynote presented in Vietnam is available for download (click here) .
